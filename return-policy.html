Return Policy

30-Day Satisfaction Guarantee

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be unused, in original packaging, and in resalable condition.

Eligible Items

Most items can be returned within 30 days of delivery. Seasonal items must be returned before the season ends.

Non-Returnable Items

Personal care products, opened items, and custom branded products cannot be returned unless defective.

Return Process

1. Contact us with your order number
2. We provide a return label (if eligible)
3. Ship items in original packaging
4. Refund issued within 5-7 business days

Defective Products

Defective items may be returned at any time. Contact us with photos for immediate replacement or full refund.
