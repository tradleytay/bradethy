Shipping Policy

Shipping Rates

Shipping charges vary based on distance and order size. Contact us for a shipping quote.

Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Custom branding orders may require additional 3-5 business days.

Domestic Shipping

Standard delivery: 3-5 business days
Express delivery: 1-2 business days

International Shipping

International delivery: 7-14 business days. Customs fees and taxes are the responsibility of the buyer.

Order Tracking

Tracking information is sent via email once your order ships. Use the tracking number on the carrier's website.

Shipping Restrictions

We ship to most countries. Some items may have shipping restrictions based on local regulations.
